Design Principles

Evolution of the EOS Brand Identity.

V1-V4: The Foundation

Focused on "Nordic Sunrise" and "Lucid Dawn". Established the Mineral Base and the use of Deep Slate for structure. Minimalism was the primary driver.

V5: Emotional Calm

Adopting the Studio Ghibli 60:30:10 profile. The Accent color (#FF2E00) is elevated to the primary navigator, creating a sense of "Infrared" warmth and intentionality in every heading.
