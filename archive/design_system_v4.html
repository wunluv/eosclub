60% Dominant (Base)

Sets the mood. Used for backgrounds, negative space, and large surfaces. Grounded, calm, mineral.

30% Secondary (Contrast)

Adds depth and structure. Used for typography, borders, navigation, and secondary buttons.

10% Accent (Highlight)

Draws the eye. Used strictly for primary CTAs, active states, and critical notifications.
